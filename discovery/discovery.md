# Discovery 1: The 60% Death Rate Illusion

Our parameter search revealed that our reaction-diffusion physics 
naturally produces either:
- 0% death rate (paradise worlds)
- 100% death rate (dead worlds)  
- ~33% death rate (sustainable worlds)

The "ideal" 60% death rate we hypothesized appears to be 
mathematically improbable in our current physics.

**Accepted Parameters:**
- metabolism: 0.65
- consumption: 7.0  
- reproduction_threshold: 77
- reproduction_cost: 87
- Expected death rate: ~33%
